Game Of Life Survive

Press and hold to draw

Speed Control

Min: 1 Max: 100

You may change the rules of survive in below ~

1. If a box has life and it has less than neighbors. It dies of loneliness. The box become lifeless next generation.
2. If a box has life and it has more than neighbors. It dies of overpopulation. The box become lifeless next generation.
3. If a box has life and it has 2 - 3 neighbors. Nothing changes for this box.
4. If a box has no life and it has neighbors, the box become with life next generation because of reproduction.
